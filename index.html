About

I am a project manager working in engineering-driven environments with a focus on project delivery, organizational structure, and operational improvement.

I hold a BSc in Industrial Engineering from Bilkent University and an MSc in Sustainable Environment and Energy Systems from Middle East Technical University.

My professional experience spans project management, logistics, energy trading, and quality systems. I currently lead hardware-oriented R&D and production projects in the defense industry.

On this site I share practical reflections on organizational improvement, project management, and personal development.
